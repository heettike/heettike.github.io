product — $1000 show

the $1000 show

too many hackathons on "ai", agents, or "shipping" — none on making money. we let 60 people do literally anything to make $1,000 in a 48-hour livestreamed hackathon. people traded perps, became tutors, sold t-shirts, launched tokens. we only planned this a week before, but regardless was fun.

product — permissionless launchpad

permissionless launchpad

we had raised $500,000 & gotten 20M eyeballs because people could bet on noice. the thought of millions of founders (who can't raise VC), tokenizing themselves to accelerate their journey never left our head.

anyone can deploy a token, no approval window. permissionless launches on base. tokens start as attention coins and graduate to ownership coins when the community grows.
