The Options

5 All-In Theses

Each is a complete pivot. Pick one.

01
PMF or Die
95% of tokens die anyway. Make it a 7-day ceremony.
0 $NOICE
Staked on this
Insight

All tokens die anyway. We bridge the ceremony to the 7th day itself. If you can't be alive by then, end it. Don't continue. The launch is well weighted in crypto—a bad launch is hard to resurrect from. Only tokens that find market fit should become ownership.

The Pitch

"7 days. $5M. Graduate or die."

How It Works
  • 5-7 launches every Monday at 8pm, starting at $125K
  • 7 day window: hit $5M mcap by day 7 or you're dead
  • Graduate = token converts to equity-grade (25% of C Corp goes to Street SPV)
  • Die = token delisted, fees go to $NOICE buyback/burn
  • Fees in escrow until day 7. Win = founder keeps all. Lose = holders get airdrop
  • Post-graduation: 1.6% to founder, 0.4% to Noice + Solana pool seeded
  • No presales. Only fair launches. Teams hold 0 coins at start
Upside

Clear narrative CT can rally behind. Creates drama and event energy. Separates signal from noise. The "PMF or Die" show could be massive content.

02
Attention Marketplace
All coins are priced on attention anyway. Index on it.
0 $NOICE
Staked on this
Insight

All coins are a proxy for mindshare. Bitcoin = digital gold. Pump = memes. Hyperliquid = perps. No matter the fundamentals—stocks, crypto, commodities—they're all priced beyond utility. The "beyond" is attention, urgency, and narrative. If all coins are priced on attention, why not embrace that and make it a strength?

The Pitch

"Distribution is the only moat. Speculation hijacks attention. We index on it."

How It Works
  • Launch coins at $125K mcap
  • Teams and investors hold 0 coins at launch
  • The fundamental reasoning: bring attention to the product & business
  • All fees flow to a marketplace of creators, devs, designers, clippers
  • Not about capital formation—about virality and distribution
  • Supply side is easy: every service person wants contracts
Upside

Reframes the narrative from "raise money" to "get distribution." Aligns incentives with what actually matters for early startups. Creates a marketplace flywheel.

03
Prediction Markets for Startups
Speculation is net positive for businesses. Make it focused.
0 $NOICE
Staked on this
Insight

Tokens have caveats: if they fall 80%, your odds of raising are hurt. A perpetual token sounds good—you IPO, people speculate—but 90% of the time it's a liability. Founders have to pump the chart instead of building product. But prediction markets? They're limited-time, focused, and don't peg your company's valuation.

The Pitch

"Monthly markets on company milestones. Simple yes/no. Will they hit it?"

How It Works
  • Founders set monthly targets: increase MRR by 40%, hire 3 engineers, launch product X
  • Any KPI is fair game—as long as you show current status + goal
  • Simple yes/no market on each milestone
  • Limited-time speculation that doesn't peg your mcap
  • Still gives distribution if done well (noisy founders win)
Target Users

YC or a16z batches. Solo app devs popular on Twitter (Blake with Cal.ai, Jack Friks). New-age startups that need to bootstrap distribution, get quality eyeballs, and go viral. You just need 1-2 prediction markets to go off.

Concern

Will it get enough liquidity? Will it actually become a distribution cheat code for startups? Unknown until proven.

04
Oracle (Like-to-Buy)
Put your money where your like is.
0 $NOICE
Staked on this
Insight

Trillions of likes on the internet. What if you could buy tokens when you pay attention (i.e., like) to takes around them? The slickest UX way to buy crypto. If we take this to 100K+ followers, we can control some mindshare for tokens—and all tokens are a proxy for mindshare anyway.

The Pitch

"Like a tweet. Buy the coin. That simple."

How It Works
  • Oracle is a Twitter page—like or comment on its tweet to buy the coin
  • Already built: agent.noice.so / user logs in on X → gets $1 on Base and Solana → sets up like-to-buy amounts → connects Telegram for notifs
  • Can create 9 accounts, each representing a category of assets
  • Works for anything onchain with a contract address
Asset Categories
  • Immediate: Pump, Clanker, Virtuals new coins (tweet metadata on launch)
  • Polymarket: "Trump wins? Like to buy YES." Elections, sports, culture
  • Onchain stocks: NVDA, TSLA, AMZN, GOOGL, PLNTR, HOOD, MSTR
  • ICMs: AIXBT, automated tweeting, LLMs on top
  • Later: RWAs, tokenized treasuries, gold, real estate, index tokens
Blockers

Twitter Enterprise access—currently only interacting with one account at a time is limiting. Ideally like-to-buy works across anyone's tweets, not just our accounts. Price updates also need solving (constant comment updates?).

05
Futarchy
Copy MetaDAO. Put it on Base/Ethereum.
0 $NOICE
Staked on this
Insight

MetaDAO built something real: market-driven governance where token holders actually own the business. They have smart talent and sophisticated capital rallying behind them. Steal like an artist. Copy the model, bring it multi-chain.

The Pitch

"MetaDAO governance on Base and Ethereum. True ownership tokens."

How It Works
  • DAO LLC construct: owns 100% IP
  • Market-driven governance: liquidation, raising, contract audits—all decided by markets
  • Monthly allowance model continues to make sense
  • Allow multi-chain deployment (Base, Ethereum, not just Solana)
Upside

Proven model with real traction. Smart capital already understands and respects this. Less educational overhead—just execute better and be multi-chain.

Risk

Pure copy with less differentiation. MetaDAO has first-mover advantage and community. Would need to find an angle beyond "same thing, different chain."
