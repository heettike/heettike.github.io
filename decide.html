01
PMF or Die
95% of tokens die anyway. Make it a 7-day ceremony.
Insight

all tokens die anyway. we bridge the ceremony to the 7th day itself. if you can't be alive by then, end it. don't continue. relaunch later. the launch is well weighted in crypto. a bad launch is hard to resurrect from (but not impossible). all coins shouldn't be ownership ‚Äî only those that find token market fit should be ownership driven.

How It Works
  • 5 (or) 7 launches every monday, 8pm
  • 7 day window: if you're above $5M at the end of 7th day - you graduate to equity grade token (25% of C Corp goes to street)
  • commit 10, 25 or 50% to street SPV
  • token fees is in an 'escrow' till day7. if graduate, get all as founder. if fail, all fees goes to buying/burning noice (or airdrop to $noice holders)
  • the pmfordie.com show could slap. but can do in month2 if this takes off
  • validate demand for your project. all good if you fail, there is some reputation risk, though
  • no presales, only fair launches at $125K
  • 1-1% split fees for first 7 days, if graduated, you get 1.6 we get 0.4
  • if graduated, we also seed a solana pool for u (i talked to jon wang and pedro a lot about this recently)
  • 95% of tokens die anyway. we're just speedrunning it into a ceremony
V2 (not necessary for v1)

there could be a prediction market 48 hours before tge 'will this token graduate?'. if you hold yes till end of 48th hour, your 'yes' amount is bundled into a devbuy (but have to hold all through the 7 days. if token fails, you lose money). if you buy no & win, you get 30% of the trading fees that's supposed to go for buyback and burn on day7 death ceremony.

02
Attention Marketplace
All coins are priced on attention anyway. Index on it.
Insight

if all tokens are priced on attention in some sense, why not embrace that weakness and make it a strength? all coins are a proxy of mindshare. bitcoin, digital gold. pump, memes. hyperliquid, perps. and so on. no matter what the fundamentals to weigh stock, crypto, commodities or memes are ‚Äî they're all priced beyond fundamentals (and) utility. the "beyond" is usually attention / urgency and narrative.

Why This

distribution is the only moat and software increases by an order of magnitude. this is a line you've read in a million tweets. and that's true. 11 products will look the same, and the one with highest distribution/virality will likely win. and speculation can hijack attention. if all coins are priced on attention anyway, why not index on it?

How It Works
  • launch coins at $125K mcap
  • teams hold 0 coins (so do investors)
  • the fundamental reasoning of the coin: bring attention to the product & business
03
Prediction Markets for Startups
Speculation is net positive for businesses. Make it focused.
Insight

speculation is net +ve for businesses. there's a multi-billion opportunity there. think acquired, angelist, polymarket and so on. but speculation comes in many forms.

Tokens and their caveats
  • if they fall 80%, your odds of raising are hurt. and it will fall 80% because: capital allocation is important, narratives (and liquidity) shifts. macro tailwinds affect things
  • a perpetual token is good; you IPO / people can speculate. but 90% of the times it's a liability. "pump the chart" is the request, founders have to do things to increase price (instead of focusing on the product)
Prediction Markets

monthly markets on companies. they decide targets: increase MRR by 40%. hire 3 employees. launch XYZ product. scale XYZ to 10,000 users. any KPI is fair game. any. as long as you tell the current status (and) the goal. limited time speculation, doesn't peg your company's mcap but STILL, gives distribution enough (if done well, if you're a noisy founder it would work).

Target Users

this could 100% convince a YC or a16z batch or solo-app-devs-that-are-popular on twitter (blake w cal ai, or jack friks) to invite speculation on their short-term, focused progress. it's a simple yes/no market.

Concern

will it get enough liquidity? will it *actually* become a distribution cheat code for startups? imo you just need 1-2 prediction markets to go off. new age startups are a new asset class which haven't found a way to a) bootstrap distribution b) get quality eyeballs c) go viral (speculation can 10x the quickness)

04
Oracle (Like-to-Buy)
Put your money where your like is.
Insight

put your money where your like is. trillions of likes on the internet, buy tokens when you pay attention (ie, like) to takes around it.

Opportunity

the slickest UX way to buy crypto. if we take this to 100K+ followers, we can control some mindshare for tokens ‚Äî and all tokens are a proxy for mindshare anyway.

Traction

5,862 users have spent $472K total in the last month, across 20 tweets. we know this like-to-buy can be fkn cool for newly launched tokens (and) buying coins when given a thesis.

How It Works

oracle is a twitter page where everytime you like or comment on its tweet, you can buy the coin. we already have the terminal: agent.noice.so / user logs in on X -> they get $1 on base and solana -> they are prompted to setup their amounts for like to buy -> connect telegram -> get notifs when u do smth.

right now it's just x.com/noiceagent ‚Äî where we manually tweet stuff about some coins (hence the cadence is off). but here is a universe of possibilities: we can create 9 accounts, each representing something that *actually* has a ticker. we won't launch all 9 at once. it only makes sense if it's working on 1. we can basically do this like-to-buy thing for *any* category of asset that is 1) onchain 2) has a contract address tied to it.

Asset Categories
  • crypto tokens about startups and icms - aixbt, automate it, tweet more, run more manual LLMs on top
  • onchain stocks (nvda, tsla, amzn, googl, plntr, hood, mstr) - summarize bloomberg terminal summaries
  • (immediate opportunity) pump, clanker or virtuals new coins - tweet standard metadata: "$noice launched on @clankeronbase. website: noice.so. mcap: $43K. launch tweet: (undefined)."
  • polymarket - Polymarket outcomes (elections, sports, crypto events, culture). each market has a contract, clear binary outcomes, and high engagement hooks. ("Trump wins? Like to buy YES"). Like and Unlike to buy No.
  • (perhaps later) RWAs - Tokenized treasuries (Ondo USDY, Mountain USDM, Backed bIB01), Tokenized gold (PAXG, XAUT), Tokenized real estate (RealT, Tangible)
  • index Tokens / ETFs - DeFi indices (DPI, MVI), AI agent baskets, Memecoin indices. Like to buy the sector, not pick the winner
  • tweet comments from fomo / clicker / interface about all the tokens they serve - standard format: "@heettike on interface "$abcd to the moon its so fkn good" at $4.77M mcap"
Issues
  • current prices: users need to know the current prices. what's the best way out here? one is to constantly keep commenting under the tweet every minute with a new price update (while deleting the last comment)
  • streaming content: imo there are APIs for every market here. could solve for all issues (according to me)
Content Strategy/Style
  • APIs and LLMs and AI yapping for each market: give a character to each agent
  • quote tweet the top 3-4 comments for a list of ~100 tickers (imagine QT'ing mert each time he talks on zcash. it would look like "@0xMert on $ZEC"). we would need to listen to all tweets on twitter, and then filter them basis the cashtag (eg, $ZEC)
